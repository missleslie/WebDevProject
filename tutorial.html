How to knit

Prepare your needles and yarn. Today you are going to learn how to make a scarf, or once you get the gist of it you can turn it into whatever you like.

Step one

Create a loop with the yarn, the pass one end through the loop creating a knot.

After creating this knot, pass the first needle through it. Ensure the knot it fully pulled and tight.Then, take the second needle and pass it throught that same loop.

Step two

Take the end of one part of the string and pass it through the middle or the gap of the both needles. And that new gap you have created with the string, pass one of the needles through.

Step three

Pull on it until a bigger and tighter knot is created. Within that knot you created there will be a small hole big enough for you to pass the needle through. Whilst doing this take a piece a string and pass it through the middle of the two needles. Continue doing the repetitively and you will start to see a pattern starting to grow on one side of the needle. Well done you have created your first step!
